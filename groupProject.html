Panther Plates

A visual journey through the world of food and culture. This group project was brought to you by: Apofiah, Jaali, Tyrin, India, Djamila, Darianne & Aboubacar.

Celebrating Food, Culture, and Connection

For this group project, we aim to showcase the diverse and vibrant world of food through a collection of images that highlight various cuisines, flavors, and presentation styles from around the globe. Our goal is to celebrate the cultural significance of food, explore the creativity behind different dishes, and bring attention to the rich history and traditions associated with what we eat. From savory street food to elegant fine dining, this project will feature a wide array of food types, offering a visual feast that appeals to the senses and sparks curiosity about the culinary arts.

Each image tells a story, whether it's a home-cooked family recipe passed down through generations or a modern, innovative dish that challenges traditional boundaries. By featuring foods from different regions, we hope to highlight how ingredients, techniques, and cultural practices shape the way we experience food and bring people together. Ultimately, this project is not just about the food itself, but about the shared human experience that it represents — fostering connection, appreciation, and understanding across cultures.

Our Mission
